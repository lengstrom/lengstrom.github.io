Gravity in Problematic Particles
# Introduction

One of the largest problems I tackled when creating the game Prolematic Particles was that of the gravitational relationship between particles and objects of mass. That problem should become immediately clear after playing with the following simulation - the particles simply do not stay in stable orbits, which makes for an extremely frustrating time trying to manipulate them into moving in any direction, such as towards a goal or away from a wall.
